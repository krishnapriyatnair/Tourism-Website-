Thiruvananthapuram

About District

Thiruvananthapuram (or Trivandrum),the capital city of Kerala,is the jewel in the emerald necklace that Kerala is for the Indian sub-continents.Being India's largest city in the deep south,Trivandrum is an appealing blend of a strongly rooted heritage and a nostalgic colonial legacy.Thiruvananthapuram also known as the "Evergreen City of India", is a swanky metropolis with quaint urban charm and plenty of scenic places to visit. Proudly retaining its age-old cultural charm, Trivandrum offers a huge variety of sights including incredible museums, beautifully designed palaces, sacred temples and mesmerizing beaches, making the city one of the best tourist hub in South India.
